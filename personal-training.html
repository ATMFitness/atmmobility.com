One-on-One Training

PERSONAL TRAINING

Strength, conditioning, and movement — built around your goals, your schedule, and your body. At our North York center or mobile across the GTA.

What to Expect

TRAINING BUILT FOR YOU

No generic programs. Anthony takes the time to understand where you're starting from, what you're working toward, and what's standing in your way — then builds a plan around that.

  • Initial assessment to establish your baseline and set realistic goals
  • Strength and conditioning programs tailored to your level
  • Weight loss, muscle building, mobility, or performance focus
  • Ongoing programming adjustments as you progress
  • Accountability and coaching every session
  • Available at our North York studio or mobile at your home
Mobile sessions available. If coming to us isn't always practical, Anthony can bring the session to you. Most equipment-light training is available across the GTA. Travel fee applies.
