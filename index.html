YOUR CENTER. YOUR SPACE.

ATM Mobility & Therapy is first and foremost a wellness center — a professional facility where clients train, recover, and feel their best. We have a dedicated training studio, a private treatment room, and full equipment for every service.

We also come to you. For clients who prefer home visits — including neurodiverse youth, those with mobility challenges, or busy schedules — most services are available across the GTA. Travel fee applies.
