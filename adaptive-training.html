ADAPTIVE TRAINING

Patient, sensory-aware training for neurodiverse youth — built around your child's unique needs, comfort, and pace.

Who We Serve

BUILT FOR EVERY LEARNER

At ATM Mobility & Therapy, we believe every young person deserves access to movement, fitness, and the confidence that comes with it. Our adaptive training program is designed for neurodiverse youth who thrive with structure, patience, and a sensory-aware approach.

Autism Spectrum ADHD Sensory Processing Developmental Coordination Down Syndrome
  • Sessions structured around your child's learning style and sensory tolerance
  • Low-pressure, distraction-minimized environment at our center
  • Flexible pacing — we follow your child's lead every session
  • Functional movement skills that transfer to daily life
  • Available at our GTA wellness center or mobile at your home
  • Parent involvement welcome — we work as a team
Pricing is customized based on session frequency, duration, and whether sessions are at our center or mobile. Contact us for a free consultation.
